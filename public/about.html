How We Started

After noticing packaging supplies overflowing from dumpsters, we set out to create an eco-friendly solution for thrifting outerwear. By featuring only local options, customers maximize sustainability options by upcycling secondhand coats, cutting down on excess packaging, and eliminating return supplies by trying on items one-on-one with Chicago sellers.

By utilizing Facebook Marketplace, our database provides up-to-date inventory for all of your winter weather needs.
